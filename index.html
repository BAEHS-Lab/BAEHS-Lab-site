Ben-Abraham Center for Environmental Health Sciences (BAEHS)

Environmental toxicology • Neurobiology • Exposure science

About the Lab

BAEHS studies environmental exposures and their effects on brain health, neurodevelopment, and population-level disease outcomes using toxicology, epidemiology, and neuroscience frameworks.

Research Focus

  • Arene oxide and reactive metabolite neurotoxicity
  • PFAS and persistent organic pollutants
  • Pesticide exposure and neurodegeneration
  • Computational toxicology and SAR/QSAR modeling
